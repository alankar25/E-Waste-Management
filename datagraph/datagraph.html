About E-Waste

Electronic waste (e-waste) is a growing problem worldwide. It includes discarded electronic devices like smartphones, laptops, and appliances. E-waste contains hazardous materials that can harm the environment if not managed properly.

E-Waste Statistics

The Impact of E-Waste

E-waste contributes to pollution, resource depletion, and health risks when not handled responsibly. It's crucial to recycle and dispose of electronic devices properly to mitigate these problems.

How You Can Help

You can make a difference by recycling your old electronics, supporting e-waste recycling programs, and raising awareness about the importance of responsible e-waste management.
